How to Use Our Products

Tongue Cleaners:

Stick your tongue out.

Put the scraper on the back of your tongue.

Pull it forward gently to get the gunk off.

Rinse the scraper and do it a few times.

Rinse your mouth when you're done.

Floss Picks:

Hold the pick.

Put the floss between your teeth.

Move it around each tooth.

Slide it up and down to get the yucky stuff off.

Use a clean part of the floss for each tooth.

Do it every day for the best results!
